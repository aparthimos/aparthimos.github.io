Baja SAE · Mechanical Design · FEA

Steering System Redesign

A full ground-up redesign of the Baja SAE steering system. The previous design relied on welded assemblies that introduced warping and alignment issues. This redesign eliminates all welded joints, replacing them with machined interfaces for precise geometry control.

  • Complete elimination of welding — removes all warp-related alignment errors
  • 2-pound overall weight reduction through topology-optimised geometry
  • Reduced part count and manufacturing complexity, lowering assembly time
  • ANSYS Static Structural FEA validation at peak cornering loads
  • Improved steering feel and reduced bump-steer through refined geometry

Custom DAQ System

A bespoke data acquisition system designed and built for the Baja SAE vehicle. Captures real-world loading data that feeds directly into FEA model validation, closing the loop between simulation and physical testing.

  • Supports linear potentiometers, hall-effect sensors, thermocouples, pressure transducers, IMUs, and accelerometers
  • CAN Bus and SMBUS protocol support for integration with existing vehicle electronics
  • ESP32-based wireless data streaming over WiFi to a remote logging laptop
  • Data used to validate FEA boundary conditions and loading assumptions
  • Custom PCB layout designed for packaging within the vehicle frame

Wave Strain Drive

A fully 3D-printable harmonic drive achieving a 20:1 reduction ratio. Explores how far FDM printing tolerances can be pushed to replicate the high-reduction, low-backlash characteristics of precision-machined harmonic drives.

Mechanical Design · Machine Learning · Prototyping

2-Speed Helical Gearbox

A 2-speed helical gearbox where gear geometry is optimised by a machine learning model trained on pinion performance data. Combines classical gear design theory with a data-driven approach to balance strength, efficiency, and noise.

  • Helical geometry selected for reduced noise and higher load capacity over spur gears
  • Python ML model trained to predict tooth root bending stress and contact fatigue life
  • Automated loop iterates pressure angle, helix angle, and module for minimum mass
  • Full assembly modelled in SolidWorks with housing designed for FDM prototyping
  • Physical prototype used to validate gear mesh quality and bearing preload
